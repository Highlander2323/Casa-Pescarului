F.A.Q.

WHERE DO I MAKE A RESERVATION?

You can either make a reservation by calling the phone number: 0760066117 or by going to the account tab in the navigation menu, creating an account or logging into an existing one.

HOW DO I GET HERE?

Unfortunately, the only way to get to the village is by boat, but up to the point where you take the boat, you can either come by car, train or plane (Tulcea has an airport). There are two locations from where you can get a boat: Tulcea and Mahmudia. Get the locations here:

I AM HERE. NOW WHAT?

Once you arrive in the village, you can either choose to come to the pension on foot, or order a private Tuk-Tuk for luggage carrying: 0746790802

Location: Casa Pescarului

WHAT IS THERE TO DO IN THE VILLAGE?

Along with the boat renting services and boat tours we have, there are other activities you can enjoy while staying at our pension.

  • The beach - A perfect place for relaxing or swimming.
  • Green Village - A resort with diverse activities and featuring: a pool, a soccer field, yoga classes, group reading, outdoors movie theatre, and many more!
  • Fishing - You can rent a rod if you do not have one and have a taste of how it feels to fish in the Danube Delta.

HOW DO I GET TO THE BEACH?

You can either choose to walk to the beach, a merely one and a half kilometer away from the village center, or get a ride from the center for only 3 RON per person.
