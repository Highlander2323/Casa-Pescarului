CASA PESCARULUI "LA MAXEA"

SERENITY, TRANQUIL, BEAUTY

Enjoy the pureness of the nature and relax in an unforgetable experience in the village where the Saint George branch greets the Black Sea, one of the most beautiful places on the face of the Earth.

With a great selection of the tastiest local dishes and the perfect place to relax, your vacation will never have felt so good. Whether you enjoy trying out good food, going on adventures or simply bathe in the warm sun whilst enjoying the breeze of the sea on your skin, Casa Pescarului has everything.
